Firearms Offence Sentencing Guide

This tool assists legal practitioners in computing indicative custodial sentences for firearms offences in accordance with structured sentencing guidelines.

The calculator walks you through a step-by-step assessment of consequence, seriousness, aggravating and mitigating factors, and any applicable guilty plea discount — producing a reasoned, documented sentencing range.

All inputs are recorded and presented in a full summary at the end. The result is indicative only and does not substitute for judicial discretion or legal advice.

Step 1 of 9Select Offence

Select the offence being evaluated from the dropdown below. You must make a selection before proceeding.

Step 2 of 9Consequence Factors

Select all that apply. Fixed-weight items are applied automatically. For ranged items, enter your chosen weighting within the stated range.

Step 3 of 9Consequence — Categories

Select one category only. Then enter a weighting within that category's range.

Step 4 of 9Seriousness Level

Select one level. Then enter a rating within that level's range.

Step 5 of 9Aggravating Factors of the Offence

Select all that apply. Each carries a fixed weighting that is automatically applied.

Step 6 of 9Mitigating Factors of the Offence

Select all that apply. Each carries a fixed weighting that is automatically applied.

Step 7 of 9Aggravating Factors of the Offender

Select all that apply. Fixed-weight items are applied automatically. For ranged items, enter your chosen weighting.

Step 8 of 9Mitigating Factors of the Offender

Select all that apply. Fixed-weight items are applied automatically. For the 5th and 6th choices, enter your chosen weighting within the stated range.

Step 9 of 9Discount for Guilty Pleas

Select one timing option. For ranged options, enter your chosen percentage discount within the stated range.
